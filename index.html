About Me

Turning complex spatial data
into decisions people trust

M.Sc. Geoinformatics · PMP® · MIET

Geospatial Intelligence for Better Decisions

I'm a senior geospatial specialist with ten years of progressive experience delivering enterprise GIS solutions across the public and private sectors — utilities, aviation, government, and FMCG. I hold an M.Sc. in Geoinformatics, a Bachelor of Honours in Survey Engineering, and PMP certification, and I'm currently progressing toward MRICS chartership.

My strength is bridging the technical and the strategic: designing unified geodatabases under demanding client standards, applying remote sensing to de-risk investment decisions, and converting thousands of engineering drawings into structured spatial assets. On the leadership side, I've directed a workforce of 60+ geospatial professionals — setting governance frameworks and delivering complex programs on schedule and within budget.

What drives me is the moment spatial data changes a conversation — when leadership stops debating opinions and starts reviewing evidence.
